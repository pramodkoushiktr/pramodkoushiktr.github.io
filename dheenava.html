Birth Youtube

It was a the result week of Kishor Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana (KVPY) exam. My JEE mathematics teacher was lost in his teaching, but I was lost in my thoughts of my first failure. Yes, I consider this as my first fail just because it was my second hard work i ever did in my life. I used to watch the drivers of a famous tour travel bus depot beside my window in the class. It was my motivation to know how is life of a common man who don't make it to IIT. I had worked really hard, and fortunately the marks i recieved was only 15.

In the same month i had also read the book 'The Man Who Knew Infinity' by Robert Kanigel. It was not just a book for me, it was the message from god to me. It was a phase of my life where there was no phone or computer for myself. I had no instagram accounts to watch a motivational porn.
