Books

Books are my refuge,they quiet everything around me and make the world fade into stillness. In those moments, I feel a kind of freedom that nothing else gives. I don’t believe in restricting what one should read. Every genre has something to offer and exploration matters more than boundaries. Reading lets me step away from the chaos of human stupid brain and enter a space that feels clearer, calmer and more genuine.

Every year list of my favourite books will be listed here. Only those that I rate 8 or more out of 10 will be falling to my favourite book list.
